Our Story

Why Bajrang Bhajiya is Mehsana's Favourite Farsan Shop

Nestled inside Hungerland Food Court on Modhera Road, Bajrang Bhajiya and Gathiya has become the go-to destination for authentic Gujarati snacks in Mehsana and the surrounding areas of Dediyasan, Brahmani Nagar, and beyond.

Every morning, our kitchen comes alive before sunrise. We freshly prepare our batter, grind our spices, and fry each batch of bhajiya to golden perfection — just like a traditional Gujarati home kitchen, but for all of Mehsana. From the iconic aloo bhajiya and mirchi bhajiya to crispy gathiya, fafda-jalebi, sev, and more, every bite carries the warmth of Gujarati heritage.

Serving the people of Mehsana, Visnagar, Kheralu, Unjha, and surrounding villages — we are proud to be your neighbourhood farsan shop.
